Centenary

Oron Union Marks 100 Years: A Historic Centenary Congress

November 17, 2025

On the occasion of its 100th anniversary, the Oron Union convened an extraordinary Congress in Oron City, Akwa Ibom State, bringing together over two thousand delegates, traditional rulers, and dignitaries from across Nigeria and the global diaspora. The event, held at the Oron Community Centre on November 15–17, 2025, marked a century of unity, resilience, and communal service since the Union's founding on May 23, 1925.

The President-General traced the arc of the Union from 88 founding patriots to a global network spanning five continents. Cultural performances, a historic exhibition of artefacts from the Oron Museum, and a grand durbar featuring masquerades and traditional dances celebrated the depth of Oro heritage. The Congress also adopted a new development blueprint — the ‘Oro Vision 2050’ — charting a bold course for economic empowerment, education, and infrastructural investment for the next generation of the Oro Nation.
