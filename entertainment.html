Annual Festivals

Three celebrations that define the Oro cultural calendar

Obon Festival

Date to be announced

The Obon Festival is the Oro Nation's most celebrated cultural event, featuring masquerades, traditional music, royal durbars, and communal feasting.

Uduk Isong Harvest

Date to be announced

A thanksgiving celebration rooted in Oro agrarian traditions. Communities present first fruits to the elders, accompanied by traditional songs and communal prayer.

Founders Day Congress

Date to be announced

The Oron Union gathers to commemorate its founding in 1925 with awards, reflection and renewal of commitment to the Union's mission.
