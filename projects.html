Community Initiatives

Long-term programmes anchoring the Union's development mission

Education Initiative

100 by 100 Scholarship Programme

A generational commitment: 100 scholars supported per year for 100 years. The 100 by 100 Programme has already funded over 500 university graduates and continues to invest in the academic futures of Oro sons and daughters at institutions across Nigeria and the wider world.

Health Initiative

Zero Malaria Oron

A targeted public health campaign in partnership with state health authorities and international donors. The initiative provides free insecticide-treated mosquito nets and rapid malaria testing for 20,000 households, with community health educators deployed across all Oro LGAs.

Economic Initiative

Oro SME Support Fund

A N50 million revolving micro-credit facility providing low-interest loans to Oro-owned small and medium enterprises. The fund has supported over 300 businesses since its launch in 2022, generating new employment and building economic resilience in Oro communities.
